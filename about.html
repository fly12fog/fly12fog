Our Story

Blackstone & Associates was founded in 2005 by James Blackstone, a solicitor with a passion for employment justice and workers' rights. What began as a small practice has grown into one of Scotland's most respected employment law firms.

Our mission has remained unchanged since our founding: to provide expert legal representation to workers facing unfair treatment in the workplace, and to ensure that every client receives the compensation and justice they deserve.

Over the past 18 years, we have successfully represented thousands of clients in cases ranging from unpaid wages and trial shifts to complex discrimination and unfair dismissal claims. Our team has expanded to include specialists in various aspects of employment law, allowing us to offer comprehensive legal services to workers across Scotland.
